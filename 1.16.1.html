Sidebar #08

Fixed several issues with netherite items being lost in fire or lava. (MCPE-70774, MCPE-79753, MCPE-84796) Recipe Select button prompt shows when hovering over a non-craftable recipe book item using controller. (MCPE-79725) Recipe selected items put in the crafting input grid can now be hovered over with the controller to see their tooltip names. Fixed a crash that could occur when playing an emote and suspending the game. (MCPE-73235) Fixed a crash that occurred on PlayStation 4 due to other players using custom skins. Emotes can no longer be equipped before unlocking them. (MCPE-84810) Custom skins once again work properly on multiplayer. (MCPE-48207)
